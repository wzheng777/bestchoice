Eye Clinic Healthcare Project

Mishe Optometry

Specialized millwork and cabinet work designed for a modern optometry practice environment.

This Metro Vancouver optometry build features custom cabinetry, display casework, and millwork designed for optical retail and patient care.

Project Overview

Mishe Optometry represents one of our most comprehensive eye care facility projects. This installation encompasses every aspect of the clinic's interior millwork, from the welcoming reception area to the specialized examination rooms and optical retail displays.

The design philosophy centered on creating a modern, clean aesthetic that reflects the precision and professionalism of optometric care. Custom-designed eyewear display cases feature integrated LED lighting and adjustable shelving to showcase frames in their best light.

Special attention was given to the examination room cabinetry, with built-in storage for diagnostic equipment and instruments. The reception desk was designed as a statement piece, combining functionality with an impressive visual presence that sets the tone for the entire patient experience.
