NeuroFocus Clinic

Custom physiotherapy furniture and cabinetry solutions designed for rehabilitation and wellness centers.

The NeuroFocus Physiotherapy project showcases custom clinic cabinetry and rehabilitation millwork crafted in Metro Vancouver for therapist and patient comfort.

Project Overview

The NeuroFocus Physiotherapy Clinic project demonstrates our capability to create specialized millwork for rehabilitation environments. This extensive installation includes treatment room cabinetry, reception areas, storage solutions, and custom furniture designed to support the unique needs of physiotherapy practice.

Understanding the importance of accessibility and functionality in rehabilitation settings, we designed each piece with careful consideration for patient mobility, therapist workflow, and equipment storage. The treatment room cabinets feature easy-access storage for therapy supplies and equipment.

The warm wood tones and clean design create a calming environment that supports patient recovery while maintaining the professional atmosphere expected in healthcare settings. Every detail was considered to ensure durability and ease of maintenance in this high-use environment.
